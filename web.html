About us

                    Lay's is a well-known brand of potato chips (crisps) manufactured by Frito-Lay, a division of PepsiCo, Inc.
                    The brand was first introduced in 1932 and has since become one of the most popular and recognized potato chip brands worldwide. 
                    Lay's offers a wide variety of flavors, catering to different tastes and preferences.
                
                    Over the years, Lay's has continuously expanded its product line to include not only traditional potato chips but also other snack products like kettle-cooked chips,
                    baked chips, and various regional and international flavors.
